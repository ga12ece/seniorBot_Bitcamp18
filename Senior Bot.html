What is Senior Bot?
Senior Bot is an interactive chat bot aims at helping senior citizens with simple e-tasks such as opening an email or Facebook account, or providing a brief description of current tech devices. The assistance can be in short answers or suggestion list. If the user requires more detailed assistance, user can request to talk to near-by experts. The near-by experts are volunteers whose basic profiles such as name, years of technical experiences and number of recent helping case are listed for senior to come get help in person if needed. When the senior sends a request to meet the expert through the chat box, a text will be sent to the expert's phone. The location from the senior to the expert is marked on an interactive map with GPS coordinate.
